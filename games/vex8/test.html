About

Vex 8, an exciting new sequel to the Vex platformer series, is here on MacVG! Vex is a very interesting and unique platformer game where you control the player to do all kinds of movements and stunts to move forward, dodge obstacles, collect coins, and reach the end! Do your best to move forward and use checkpoints to save your progress! There's many levels to choose from and enjoy. Have fun!

Controls

  • Up Arrow: Jump
  • Down Arrow: Slide / Dodge
  • Left / Right Arrows: Move Left / Right
  • Note: WASD Keys work the same.
